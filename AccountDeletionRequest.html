Account Deletion Request

Request Account Deletion

We respect your privacy and your right to have your personal data deleted. This page explains how to request the deletion of your account and all associated data from our system.

How to Request Account Deletion

Email us at:
deletion@barberlabkonstantinos.com

Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

Required Information

Please include the following information in your email:

  1. Phone number used for registration
  2. Email address (if you used one)
  3. Full name as registered
  4. Reason for deletion (optional but helpful)

What Will Be Deleted

  • Personal information (name, phone, email)
  • Account credentials and login details
  • Appointment history and booking records
  • FCM tokens and notification preferences
  • User preferences and settings
  • All associated data and metadata

Processing Timeline

Response time: We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours

Deletion completion: All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Important Notes

⚠️ Irreversible Action: Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

Data Retention: Some information may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes as required by law.

Third-party Services: If you used Google Sign-In, you may need to manage your Google account settings separately.

Alternative Options

Before requesting complete deletion, consider these alternatives:

  • Account Deactivation: Temporarily disable your account
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy preferences

If you'd like to explore these options, please contact us at the same email address.
